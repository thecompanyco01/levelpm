Detailed Reviews

1. AppFolio — Best for Growing PM Companies (200+ Doors)

⭐ 4.5/5

AppFolio is the most popular PM software for mid-size companies. It's feature-rich, constantly innovating (AI leasing assistant, smart maintenance), and has the best mobile experience in the category.

Pricing: $1.40/unit/month for Core (minimum $280/month). Plus plan at $3/unit/month. No free trial — only demos.

✅ Pros

  • Best-in-class mobile app
  • AI-powered leasing & maintenance
  • Excellent reporting & analytics
  • Integrated tenant screening
  • Strong owner portal
  • Active development & updates

❌ Cons

  • $280/month minimum is steep for small PMs
  • No free trial
  • Limited customization vs. Rent Manager
  • Per-unit pricing adds up fast
  • Some features require Plus plan

🎯 Best for:

PM companies managing 200-2,000 doors who want a modern, all-in-one platform with strong automation. If you're growing fast and need a platform that scales with you, AppFolio is the safe choice.

2. Buildium — Best Value for Mid-Size PMs (50-500 Doors)

⭐ 4.3/5

Buildium hits the sweet spot of features and affordability. It's easier to learn than AppFolio, cheaper per unit, and has solid HOA/community association support.

Pricing: Essential at $55/month (up to 20 units), Growth at $174/month (up to 50 units), Premium at $375/month (up to 250 units). 14-day free trial available.

✅ Pros

  • Free trial available
  • Lower entry price than AppFolio
  • Built-in eLease signing
  • Good HOA/association management
  • Solid accounting features
  • Easier learning curve

❌ Cons

  • Mobile app less polished than AppFolio
  • Fewer automation features
  • Reporting not as deep
  • Customer support can be slow
  • Website builder is limited

🎯 Best for:

PM companies managing 50-500 doors who want solid features without overpaying. Especially good if you also manage HOAs or community associations.

3. Rent Manager — Best for Enterprise/Complex Portfolios (500+ Doors)

⭐ 4.4/5

Rent Manager is the most powerful and customizable PM software available. If you need complex reporting, commercial property support, or deep integrations, this is your platform.

Pricing: Custom quotes only. Typically ~$1/unit/month for large portfolios. Implementation fee varies ($2,000-$10,000+).

✅ Pros

  • Most customizable PM platform
  • Excellent for mixed portfolios
  • Advanced accounting & reporting
  • Strong API & integration ecosystem
  • Dedicated account manager

❌ Cons

  • Steepest learning curve
  • High implementation cost
  • Interface feels dated
  • Not ideal for small portfolios
  • Custom quotes only (no transparent pricing)

4. TenantCloud — Best Free PM Software (Under 75 Doors)

⭐ 4.0/5

TenantCloud offers genuinely useful free-tier PM software. For small landlords or new PM companies, it's the best way to get professional tools without spending a dime.

Pricing: Free (up to 75 units), Starter at $15.60/month, Growth at $50.40/month, Pro at $35/month.

✅ Pros

  • Free tier is genuinely useful
  • Great for beginners
  • Online rent collection included
  • Built-in tenant screening
  • Nice tenant portal

❌ Cons

  • Limited reporting
  • No API
  • Basic accounting
  • Not suitable for 100+ doors
  • Owner portal is limited

5 Mistakes to Avoid When Choosing PM Software

  1. Choosing based on price alone. The cheapest option often costs more in workarounds. Consider total cost of ownership.
  2. Not getting a live demo. Screenshots don't show workflow friction. Always do a hands-on demo with YOUR data.
  3. Ignoring migration costs. Switching PM software is painful. Factor in data migration time (usually 2-4 weeks).
  4. Buying for today, not tomorrow. Choose a platform you can grow into. Switching in 2 years wastes everything you invested.
  5. Skipping the team's input. Your maintenance coordinator, property managers, and bookkeeper will use this daily. Get their buy-in.
