1. Maintenance Triage SOP

A priority matrix system for categorizing maintenance requests into Emergency, Urgent, Routine, and Scheduled. Includes step-by-step decision tree, owner notification thresholds ($500+ requires approval), after-hours protocol, and vendor dispatch guidelines.

Why you need it: Maintenance is the #1 source of tenant complaints AND owner frustration. A triage system ensures emergencies get handled in minutes, routine requests within 48 hours, and owners aren't surprised by costs.
