47 Property Management Email Templates That Save Hours Every Week

Updated March 2026 · 18 min read

The average property manager sends 50+ emails per day. That's 250+ per week, most of them repetitive — lease reminders, maintenance updates, owner reports, late rent notices. Without templates, you're rewriting the same messages over and over.

We've compiled 47 battle-tested email templates that property management companies actually use. Copy them, customize them for your brand, and start saving 5-10 hours per week.

Tenant Communication Templates

1. Welcome Email (New Tenant Move-In)

Subject: Welcome to [Property Address] — Your Move-In Guide Hi [Tenant Name], Welcome to your new home! We're excited to have you at [Property Address]. Here's everything you need for a smooth move-in: 📅 Move-in date: [Date] 🔑 Key pickup: [Location/time] 🔧 Maintenance requests: [Portal URL or email] 💰 Rent payment: [Portal URL] — due on the 1st of each month 📞 Emergency line: [Phone number] Attached: Your move-in checklist and community guidelines. Please complete your move-in inspection within 48 hours using [the attached form / our online portal]. This protects both of us. Questions? Reply to this email anytime. Best, [Your Name] [Company Name]

2. Lease Renewal Offer (60-Day Notice)

Subject: Your Lease Renewal Options at [Property Address] Hi [Tenant Name], Your current lease at [Property Address] expires on [Date]. We value you as a resident and would love to have you stay! Here are your renewal options: • 12-month renewal: $[Amount]/month (best value) • 6-month renewal: $[Amount]/month • Month-to-month: $[Amount]/month Current rate: $[Current Amount]/month Please let us know your preference by [Deadline — 30 days out]. If we don't hear from you, your lease will convert to month-to-month at the rate above. To renew, simply reply to this email or sign digitally at [Portal URL]. Thank you for being a great tenant! [Your Name] [Company Name]

3. Late Rent Notice (Friendly First Reminder)

Subject: Friendly Reminder — Rent Payment for [Month] Hi [Tenant Name], This is a courtesy reminder that your rent payment of $[Amount] for [Month] was due on [Due Date] and we haven't received it yet. If you've already sent payment, please disregard this message. To pay now: [Payment Portal URL] If you're experiencing financial difficulty, please reach out. We may be able to work out a payment plan. A late fee of $[Amount] will apply after [Grace Period Date] per your lease agreement. Thank you, [Your Name] [Company Name]

4. Maintenance Request Acknowledgment

Subject: Maintenance Request #[Number] Received Hi [Tenant Name], We've received your maintenance request: Issue: [Description] Priority: [Urgent/Standard/Low] Submitted: [Date] Next steps: • Our maintenance team will contact you within [24/48] hours • If applicable, a vendor will be scheduled to visit • You'll receive updates at each stage For urgent issues (water leaks, no heat, security concerns), call our emergency line: [Phone]. We'll keep you posted. [Your Name] [Company Name]

5. Maintenance Completed Follow-Up

Subject: Maintenance Complete — Request #[Number] Hi [Tenant Name], Your maintenance request has been completed: Issue: [Description] Completed by: [Vendor/Staff Name] Date: [Completion Date] Is everything working properly? If the issue persists or you notice anything else, reply to this email within 48 hours and we'll send someone back at no charge. Your comfort is our priority. [Your Name] [Company Name]

6. Lease Violation Notice

Subject: Important Notice — Lease Violation at [Property Address] Dear [Tenant Name], During a recent [inspection/complaint investigation], we identified a lease violation at your unit: Violation: [Specific violation — noise, unauthorized pet, etc.] Lease clause: Section [X], paragraph [Y] Date observed: [Date] Required action: [What they need to do] Deadline: [Date — typically 7-14 days] This is a formal notice, not an eviction. We want to resolve this amicably. Please address the issue by the deadline above and confirm with us once corrected. If you have questions or believe this is in error, please contact us within 5 business days. Sincerely, [Your Name] [Company Name]

7. Move-Out Instructions

Subject: Move-Out Instructions for [Property Address] Hi [Tenant Name], Thank you for the notice that you'll be vacating [Property Address] on [Date]. Here's your move-out checklist: □ Remove all personal belongings and trash □ Clean all rooms, appliances, and fixtures □ Patch small nail holes (spackle provided at office) □ Replace any burned-out light bulbs □ Return all keys, remotes, and access devices to [Location] □ Provide forwarding address for security deposit return Move-out inspection: [Date/Time] — please be present if possible. Security deposit: Will be returned within [State requirement — typically 14-30 days] minus any documented deductions. Questions? Don't hesitate to reach out. [Your Name] [Company Name]

Owner Communication Templates

Questions? Reply anytime. [Your Name] [Company Name]

9. New Owner Welcome / Onboarding

Subject: Welcome to [Company Name] — Next Steps Hi [Owner Name], Welcome aboard! We're excited to manage [Property Address] for you. Here's what happens next: Week 1: □ Management agreement signed ✓ □ Property inspection scheduled: [Date] □ Tenant notification sent (introducing us as new managers) □ Owner portal access: [URL] — login credentials attached Week 2: □ Vendor accounts transitioned □ Insurance verification □ First rent collection under our management Your dedicated property manager: [Name] — [Email] / [Phone] Owner portal: Access your financials, statements, and property updates 24/7 at [URL]. We'll send your first monthly report on [Date]. [Your Name] [Company Name]

10. Vacancy Update to Owner

Subject: Vacancy Update — [Property Address] Hi [Owner Name], Here's the latest on filling the vacancy at [Property Address]: 📊 MARKETING STATS (since [listing date]) • Listing views: [Number] • Inquiries received: [Number] • Showings completed: [Number] • Applications received: [Number] Listed on: Zillow, Apartments.com, Facebook Marketplace, [MLS] Asking rent: $[Amount]/month [If applications received:] Top applicant: [Brief — income, credit, move-in date] Recommendation: [Accept/Continue showing/Adjust price] [If slow activity:] Recommendation: [Adjust price to $X / Improve photos / Add incentive] We'll keep you updated as things progress. [Your Name] [Company Name]

Vendor & Maintenance Templates

11. Vendor Work Order

Subject: Work Order — [Property Address], Unit [X] Hi [Vendor Name], We have a maintenance request that needs your attention: Property: [Address], Unit [X] Issue: [Detailed description] Priority: [Emergency/Urgent/Standard] Tenant contact: [Name, Phone] Access instructions: [Lockbox code / tenant will be home / etc.] Budget: Not to exceed $[Amount] without prior approval Deadline: [Date] Please confirm your availability and expected arrival time. Invoice to: [Your company email/address] PO#: [If applicable] Thank you, [Your Name] [Company Name]

12. Vendor Insurance Request

Subject: Insurance Certificate Request — [Vendor Company] Hi [Vendor Name], Before we can add you to our approved vendor list, we need: □ Certificate of Insurance (COI) showing: • General liability: $1M minimum • Workers' compensation (if applicable) • [Company Name] listed as additional insured □ W-9 form □ Business license copy Please send these to [email] at your earliest convenience. Once received, we'll activate your vendor account and start sending work orders. Thank you, [Your Name] [Company Name]

I run [Company Name] — we manage [X] properties in [Area] and specialize in [niche: single-family, multifamily, etc.]. Three things we do differently: 1. [Specific differentiator — e.g., "Monthly video property inspections"] Would a quick 15-minute call be worth your time? No pressure — just want to see if we're a fit. If self-managing ever becomes more hassle than it's worth, we're here. [Your Name] [Company Name]

Thank you, [Your Name] [Company Name]

Lease & Legal Templates

16. Rent Increase Notice

Subject: Lease Update — Rent Adjustment for [Property Address] Dear [Tenant Name], Per our lease agreement and [State] law, we're providing [30/60/90]-day notice of a rent adjustment effective [Date]: Current rent: $[Amount]/month New rent: $[Amount]/month Effective date: [Date] This adjustment reflects [market conditions / increased property taxes / maintenance costs / CPI adjustment]. Your new lease terms are attached. Please sign and return by [Date]. If you have questions, we're happy to discuss. [Your Name] [Company Name]

17. Security Deposit Itemization

Subject: Security Deposit Accounting — [Property Address] Dear [Former Tenant Name], Per [State] law, here is the itemized accounting of your security deposit: Security deposit held: $[Amount] DEDUCTIONS: • [Item]: $[Amount] — [Brief description + photo reference] • [Item]: $[Amount] — [Brief description] • Cleaning: $[Amount] Total deductions: $[Amount] REFUND: $[Amount] Your refund check has been mailed to [forwarding address] on [Date]. Move-out inspection photos are attached for reference. If you dispute any deduction, please contact us within [State timeframe]. [Your Name] [Company Name]

Seasonal & Preventive Templates

18. Seasonal Maintenance Reminder (Winter)

Subject: Winter Prep Tips for Your Unit Hi [Tenant Name], Winter is coming! Here are a few quick steps to protect your home and avoid costly repairs: ❄️ YOUR CHECKLIST: □ Set thermostat to at least 65°F (even when away) □ Open cabinet doors under sinks on very cold nights □ Know where your water shut-off valve is □ Report any drafts, leaks, or heating issues immediately □ Don't use space heaters near curtains or furniture We've already winterized exterior pipes and checked the heating system. If you notice anything concerning, submit a maintenance request at [Portal URL]. Stay warm! [Your Name] [Company Name]

19. Annual Inspection Notice

Subject: Scheduled Property Inspection — [Date] Hi [Tenant Name], Per your lease agreement, we'll be conducting a routine property inspection: 📅 Date: [Date] 🕐 Time: Between [Time Range] 👤 Inspector: [Name/Company] This is a standard annual inspection to check for maintenance needs and ensure the property is in good condition. It typically takes 15-20 minutes. You're welcome to be present but it's not required. Per [State] law, this serves as your [24/48]-hour notice. If this time doesn't work, please suggest an alternative within the next 7 days. Thank you, [Your Name] [Company Name]

How to Use These Templates Effectively

Customization Tips

  • Brand them: Add your logo, colors, and company-specific language
  • Localize: Update legal references to match your state's landlord-tenant laws
  • Personalize: Use the tenant/owner's name, property address, and specific details
  • Automate: Load these into your PM software (AppFolio, Buildium, Rent Manager) as templates
  • Test: Track which subject lines get the best open rates and iterate

Email Best Practices for Property Managers

  1. Respond within 4 hours during business hours — tenants rank responsiveness as their #1 factor in satisfaction
  2. Use clear subject lines — tenants get a lot of email. "Maintenance Complete — Faucet Repair" beats "Update"
  3. Keep a paper trail — email creates documentation that protects you legally
  4. Send from a consistent address — tenants should recognize your email immediately
  5. Include next steps — every email should tell the recipient what to do next
