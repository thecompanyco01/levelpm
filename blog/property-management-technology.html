What Your PM Software Must Do

  • Online rent collection — ACH, credit card, and cash payment options
  • Tenant portal — Maintenance requests, lease documents, payment history
  • Owner portal — Financial statements, tax documents, property updates
  • Trust accounting — Separate accounts per property/owner, automated reconciliation
  • Maintenance workflow — Request → assign → track → close → invoice
  • Lease management — Electronic signatures, renewal tracking, template library
  • Vacancy marketing — Listing syndication to Zillow, Apartments.com, etc.
  • Reporting — Customizable P&L, rent roll, vacancy reports, owner statements

Communication Platforms

Response time is the #1 factor in tenant and owner satisfaction. Modern communication tools let you be responsive without being chained to your phone.

Tenant Communication

  • Tenant portal (via PM software) — Primary channel for maintenance and documents
  • Text messaging — Use platforms like Podium or Heymarket that log all messages for compliance
  • Automated notifications — Rent reminders, lease expiration notices, maintenance updates

Owner Communication

  • Owner portal with real-time financial data
  • Monthly reports — Automated from your PM software (use our Owner Report Template)
  • Video updates — Loom or similar for property walk-throughs
Pro Tip: Set up automated monthly owner emails with financial summaries, even if owners can check the portal themselves. Proactive communication is the #1 reason owners stay with a PM company. Our Owner Reporting Template gives you the exact format.

Building Your Tech Stack: A Framework

Don't buy everything at once. Layer technology as you grow:

Stage 1: 0-50 Doors

  • PM software (Buildium or TenantCloud)
  • Google Workspace (email, calendar, docs)
  • Phone camera + standardized checklists for inspections
  • Cost: $50-100/month

Stage 2: 50-200 Doors

  • Upgrade PM software if needed (AppFolio)
  • Inspection app (HappyCo or ZInspector)
  • Text messaging platform
  • Automated rent reminders and notifications
  • Cost: $300-600/month

Stage 3: 200-500 Doors

  • Full PM software suite with all integrations
  • AI maintenance triage
  • Rent pricing tools (PriceLabs)
  • CRM for owner acquisition
  • Project management (Asana/Monday.com for team coordination)
  • Cost: $800-1,500/month

Stage 4: 500+ Doors

  • Enterprise PM platform (Rent Manager or AppFolio Plus)
  • Business intelligence/dashboards
  • Custom API integrations
  • Dedicated IT support
  • Cost: $2,000+/month

Common Technology Mistakes

  1. Over-buying early. You don't need a $2,000/month tech stack at 75 doors. Start lean and add tools as pain points emerge.
  2. Under-training staff. Software is only as good as the people using it. Budget 2-4 hours of training per new tool per employee.
  3. Ignoring integrations. Your PM software, accounting, inspections, and communication tools need to talk to each other. Data silos kill efficiency.
  4. Not documenting workflows. Every tool needs an SOP for how your team uses it. Technology without process is just expensive confusion.
  5. Switching too often. Every software migration costs 2-4 weeks of disruption. Pick tools with 3-5 year staying power.
