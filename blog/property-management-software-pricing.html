We've analyzed pricing for every major PM software platform in 2026, including hidden fees that vendors don't advertise on their pricing pages. Here's the real breakdown.

Detailed Pricing Breakdown

AppFolio — Best for Growing Companies (200+ Units)

AppFolio is the market leader for mid-size PM companies. Their pricing is straightforward but has a high minimum that prices out smaller operators.

  • Core plan: $1.49/unit/month (minimum $298/month = 200 units)
  • Plus plan: $3.20/unit/month (minimum $640/month)
  • Setup fee: $400 (one-time)
  • Online payments: ACH free, credit card 2.95%
  • Tenant screening: $15–$30/screening (paid by applicant typically)
  • Electronic leases: Included

True cost at 300 units: ~$450–$960/month depending on plan

True cost at 1,000 units: ~$1,490–$3,200/month

Buildium — Best Value for Mid-Size (20–500 Units)

Buildium offers the most flexible pricing with no per-unit minimums on their Essential plan, making it accessible for smaller operators.

  • Essential: $58/month (up to 150 units)
  • Growth: $183/month (up to 150 units + premium features)
  • Premium: $375/month (unlimited units + priority support)
  • Online payments: ACH $1.25/transaction, credit card 2.95%
  • Setup fee: None
  • Tenant screening: $15/screening

True cost at 100 units: ~$58–$375/month + $125/month in ACH fees (if 100 tenants pay online)

Rent Manager — Enterprise Choice (500+ Units)

Rent Manager is the most customizable option but requires a demo and custom quote. Pricing isn't public, which is a red flag for some buyers.

  • Pricing: Custom quote only
  • Estimated range: $1–$3/unit/month based on volume
  • Deployment: Cloud or on-premise options
  • Best for: Complex portfolios, commercial + residential mixed

Propertyware — Single-Family Specialist

Owned by RealPage, Propertyware focuses exclusively on single-family rental management.

  • Basic: $1/unit/month (minimum $250/month)
  • Plus: $1.50/unit/month (minimum $375/month)
  • Premium: $2/unit/month (minimum $500/month)
  • Setup fee: 2x monthly subscription (one-time)

Budget-Friendly Options (Under 100 Units)

If you manage fewer than 100 units, you don't need AppFolio or Rent Manager. These options give you 80% of the features at 20% of the cost:

  • TenantCloud: Free up to 75 units, then $15–$50/month
  • Rentec Direct: $50/month for up to 25 units, $1/unit after
  • SimplifyEm: $25/month for up to 10 units
  • DoorLoop: $59/month starter with decent features

Hidden Fees to Watch For

The "sticker price" of PM software rarely tells the full story. Here are the fees that add up:

  1. Payment processing fees: $1–2.50 per ACH payment × hundreds of tenants = $100–500+/month
  2. Tenant screening fees: Usually passed to applicants, but some platforms charge the PM company
  3. Setup/onboarding fees: $0–$500+ depending on platform
  4. Data migration: Some charge $500–$2,000 to import your existing data
  5. Premium support: $50–$200/month for phone support or dedicated rep
  6. Add-on modules: Maintenance coordination, inspection tools, CRM features
  7. Annual price increases: Many platforms raise prices 5–15% annually
💡 Key insight: At 200 units, your all-in software cost (including payment processing) is typically $400–$1,200/month. Budget 1–2% of gross rent collected for technology costs.

The Real Cost of Switching Software

Choosing wrong costs more than the monthly fee. A software migration typically involves:

  • 2–4 weeks of staff time for data entry and training
  • $1,000–$5,000 in migration costs (data transfer, setup, training)
  • Lost productivity during the transition period
  • Tenant confusion with new payment portals and communication channels

This is why getting it right the first time matters. Don't pick software based solely on today's portfolio — pick it based on where you'll be in 2–3 years.
