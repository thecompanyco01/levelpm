When to Choose AppFolio

  • You have 200+ units and are growing quickly
  • Your team lives on their phones — AppFolio's mobile app is unmatched
  • You need AI-powered maintenance triage to reduce after-hours calls
  • Owner acquisition is a priority — AppFolio's built-in CRM helps track leads
  • You want free ACH payments (saves $100–500/month vs Buildium at scale)
  • You're okay with an annual contract commitment

When to Choose Buildium

  • You have under 200 units — Buildium's Essential plan ($58/mo) is unbeatable
  • You manage HOAs or community associations alongside rentals
  • You want month-to-month flexibility (no annual contract)
  • Budget matters — Buildium costs 50–75% less than AppFolio at most portfolio sizes
  • You want no setup fee (AppFolio charges $400)
  • You prefer to start small and scale without high minimums

The Switch Factor

Many PM companies start on Buildium and switch to AppFolio around 300–500 units. This is common and makes sense — Buildium's value proposition is strongest under 200 units, and AppFolio's automation features become worth the premium as you scale past 300.

If you're at 50–150 units right now, start with Buildium. Save thousands in the first 1–2 years, invest that money in growth (marketing, SOPs, hiring), and evaluate AppFolio when you hit 250+ units.

The Bigger Question Nobody Asks

Most PM companies spend weeks choosing between AppFolio and Buildium — but spend zero time documenting their operations. Software doesn't create good processes. It automates the ones you already have.

Before you sign up for either platform, ask yourself:

  • Do I have documented SOPs for every repeatable process?
  • Can a new hire get up to speed in under a week?
  • Are my financials clean and my owner reporting automated?
  • Do I know my cost-per-door and profit margin?

If the answer to any of those is "no," your biggest ROI isn't software — it's operational infrastructure.
