Best Free YouTube Channels for PM Training

YouTube is an underrated PM education platform. These channels are run by practicing property managers, not theorists:

1. DoorGrow Show (Jason Hull)

Best for PM company owners focused on growth. Episodes cover owner acquisition, team building, pricing strategy, and scaling. 400+ episodes covering nearly every PM business topic.

2. NARPM Education

The National Association of Residential Property Managers posts conference talks and educational content. More formal/structured than other channels, focused on compliance and best practices.

3. BiggerPockets Podcasts

While broader than just PM, BiggerPockets covers property management frequently, especially from the investor/owner perspective. Good for understanding what your clients care about.

4. Second Nature (formerly RBP)

Focuses on resident experience and modern PM practices. Good content on tenant retention, ancillary revenue, and resident benefit packages.

5. PM Software Channels

AppFolio, Buildium, and Rent Manager all maintain YouTube channels with software tutorials and industry webinars. Essential if you use their platforms.

When Free Isn't Enough

Free resources will get you 70% of the way. But there are gaps that free content doesn't fill well:

  • Production-ready SOPs — Generic templates need heavy customization. Professional SOP packages save 20-40 hours of adaptation work.
  • Scaling systems — Free content tells you what to do. Paid resources give you the exact workflows, templates, and processes to do it at scale.
  • Accountability — Free courses have low completion rates. Paid programs create commitment.
  • Community — Paid programs often include access to a peer group of other PM operators. This network is often worth more than the content.
