💡 Best Practices:
• Always do inspections with the tenant present — it prevents disputes later.
• Take 50+ timestamped photos per inspection. Organize by room in a shared folder.
• Complete move-out inspection within 24 hours of tenant vacating.
• Normal wear and tear ≠ damage. Know your state's definition before charging deposits.
• Keep completed checklists for 7+ years (check your state's statute of limitations).
